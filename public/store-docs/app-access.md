## Google Play Console — "App Access" Section

### Access Level

**All functionality is available** without requiring testers to log in or meet special conditions.

### Details

| Feature Area          | Login Required? | Notes                                         |
|-----------------------|-----------------|-----------------------------------------------|
| 3D Circuit Builder    | No              | Fully accessible without an account           |
| Ohm's Law Simulator   | No              | Fully accessible without an account           |
| Component Library     | No              | Fully accessible without an account           |
| Save / Load Circuits  | Yes             | Requires a free account                       |
| Community Hub         | Yes             | Requires a free account to post               |
| Arcade                | No              | Play without an account                       |
| Classroom Management  | Yes (Educator)  | Requires an Educator subscription             |
| Premium Content Packs | Yes (Subscriber)| Requires an active subscription               |

### Instructions for Reviewers

1. Launch the app — the 3D builder opens immediately on the home screen.
2. No login is needed to explore the circuit builder, component library, simulator, or Arcade.
3. To test account-gated features: tap **Account** in the nav bar and use one of the sample credentials below.
