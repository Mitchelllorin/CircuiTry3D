## Google Play Console — "Target Audience and Content" Section

### Primary Target Audience

**Ages 13 and older.**

CircuiTry3D is an educational tool for students, educators, and electronics enthusiasts. The primary audience includes:

- Middle and high school students (ages 13–18) studying physics, electronics, or STEM
- University and college students in electrical engineering or related fields
- CTE (Career and Technical Education) and Makerspace participants
- Teachers and educators creating interactive lessons
- Electronics hobbyists and makers of all ages

### Families Policy

CircuiTry3D is **not** enrolled in the Google Play Families programme. The app does not target children under 13 and is not listed in the Family section of Google Play.

If users under 13 access the app, parental supervision is recommended. The app:
- Does not collect personal data from users under 13 without parental consent.
- Does not display advertisements.
- Does not contain inappropriate content.
