Component Arena · FUSE™

Fail safely.  Learn deeply.

The Failure Understanding Simulation Engine™ applies realistic thermal and electrical stress models to every component — complete with smoke, arcs, and explosions.

OK
Resistor 10 kΩ
Operating at 12 % rated power — thermally stable with comfortable margin.
Stressed
Capacitor 100 µF
Approaching voltage limit — ripple current elevated, ESR warming.
Warning
LED (Red)
Forward current exceeds spec — junction heating, dimming imminent.
Failed
Fuse 0.5 A
Overcurrent event detected — element vaporized, circuit open.

Powered by FUSE™ — a first-order thermal time-constant model that simulates real-world component degradation over time.
