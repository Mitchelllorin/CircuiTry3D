Component Arena · FUSE™

Fail safely.  Learn deeply.

The Failure Understanding Simulation Engine™ applies realistic thermal and electrical stress models to all 23 component families — including wires. Exceed a wire's rated ampacity and the jacket smokes; push past insulation limits and it arcs. Complete with particle effects and physics-accurate material descriptions.

OK
Resistor 10 kΩ
Operating at 12 % rated power — thermally stable with comfortable margin.
Stressed
Capacitor 100 µF
Approaching voltage limit — ripple current elevated, ESR warming.
Warning
LED (Red)
Forward current exceeds spec — junction heating, dimming imminent.
Failed
Fuse 0.5 A
Overcurrent event detected — element vaporized, circuit open.

Powered by FUSE™ — a first-order thermal time-constant model that simulates real-world component degradation over time.

Your AI learning companion

Two AI-powered tools live directly in the circuit builder — no separate app, no extra accounts. Ask questions, get instant answers, and let AI explain exactly what your circuit is doing.

⚡
Circuit AI
Free · All Users

A real-time chat assistant built directly into the circuit builder. Ask anything — Ohm's Law, KCL/KVL, component behavior, wiring mistakes — and get context-aware answers based on what you've actually built.

  • 40+ electrical topics: theory, components, troubleshooting, app help
  • Reads your live circuit metrics and tailors answers to your build
  • Suggested questions help beginners get started instantly
  • Works offline — no internet connection required

Explain Circuit
Premium & Pro

One tap generates a full AI-powered breakdown of your live circuit. Understand why your circuit works — or why it doesn't — with a structured, plain-language report.

  • Summary — topology overview and circuit type
  • Current Flow — step-by-step path current takes
  • Component Roles — what each part is doing and why
  • Expected Behavior — predicted voltage, current, and power
  • Common Mistakes — design errors automatically flagged
