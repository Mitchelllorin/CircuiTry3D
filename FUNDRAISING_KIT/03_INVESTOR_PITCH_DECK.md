## DESIGN NOTES

**Deck Design**:
- Clean, modern, tech-forward aesthetic  
- Brand colors: Blue (#88ccff), Orange (#ff8844), Green (#00ff88)
- Use actual CircuiTry3D screenshots (not stock photos)
- Keep text minimal (use speaker notes for details)
- Include one 3D circuit animation or GIF

**Tools to Create**:
- **Pitch.com** (collaborative, beautiful templates)
- **Canva** (easy, drag-and-drop)
- **Google Slides** (simple, shareable)
- **Keynote** (if you're on Mac, very polished)

**Export Formats**:
- PDF (for email distribution)
- Docsend link (track who views, when, for how long)
- PowerPoint (for investor's internal sharing)
